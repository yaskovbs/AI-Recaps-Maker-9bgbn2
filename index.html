AI Recaps Maker

AI Recaps Maker is an AI-assisted video recap creation application. It helps creators turn video, audio, scripts, and supported links they own or are authorized to use into editable video recaps.

Users can upload source material, select a recap style and duration, process the content, review the generated result, and then download or share their recap. Google sign-in is used only to create and authenticate an AI Recaps Maker account using basic profile information authorized by the user.
